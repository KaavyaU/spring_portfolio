Our Goal

How it Works

  • The general idea of our project is for students to collect bottles independently or at meetups, and then trade them into the Del Norte office for dineros
  • The school can then exchange these bottles at one of the many businesses which take in bottles and reward money
  • Since dineros can be exchanged for cafeteria items, we will make sure that the school recieves more money than it rewards students through dineros
  • The school will also host different recycling meetups to encourage collaboration, friendships, and recycling

Purpose

  • Our project's ultimate goal is to improve the environment and provide the school with extra funding for different programs
  • By giving students an extra incentive of dineros, we can encourage students to recycle at different school meetups, which can also serve as an additional extracuriccular
  • Since the school can exchange recycled bottles for a profit at a different institution, the project will also serve as a fundraiser for more programs benefiting students and staff
